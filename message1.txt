SUBJECT: Support for Freedom and Democracy in Iran

BODY:
Dear Members of Parliament,
Dear Decision-Makers,

As a citizen of Iranian origin residing in the United Kingdom, I am writing to you regarding the ongoing struggle of the Iranian people for freedom, democracy, and the rule of law.

In light of the continued efforts of the Iranian people to achieve fundamental rights and democratic governance, I respectfully call for consistent and unwavering support from the United Kingdom for this historic transformation until the end of the Islamic Republic.

At the same time, I wish to emphasize the absolute importance of preserving Iran’s territorial integrity. Any future political order must be based on the sovereign will of the Iranian people and must comply with international legal standards.

To ensure a stable transition period and to prevent a power vacuum, I highlight the coordinating role of Crown Prince Reza Pahlavi as a potential leader of a transitional administration. His leadership could play an important role in representing the aspirations of many Iranians and guiding a peaceful democratic transition.

As demonstrated in recent years, and particularly through the protests both inside Iran and among the Iranian diaspora worldwide, many Iranians support democratic change and seek a future based on freedom, human rights, and the rule of law.

I respectfully urge you, within the scope of your mandate, to encourage the United Kingdom to adopt a clear political stance in support of the freedom, human rights, and democratic aspirations of the Iranian people.

Sincerely,
